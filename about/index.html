We are passionate about mental health

A bootstrapped startup based in Bengaluru, we are a team of passionate, experienced techies, business professionals, doctors, designers and psychologists who believe that mental health is arguably one of the most important healthcare problems to solve in the 21st century.

Our first product, a custom-built solution to help work professionals become mentally more productive, will launch in August 2023.

Lightner Witmer

Psychologist

Our name is a tribute to Lightner Witmer (1867-1956), a famous American psychologist. He introduced the term “clinical psychology” and is often credited with founding the field that it describes. Witmer created the world’s first “psychological clinic” at the University of Pennsylvania in 1896, the first journal of clinical psychology and the first clinical hospital school in 1907.

Witmer contributed to numerous branches of psychology including school psychology. He also made significant contribution to the field of special education.

Source: Wikipedia
