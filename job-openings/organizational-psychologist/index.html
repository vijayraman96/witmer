QA Automation Tester

Company Overview:

At Witmer we believe in transforming workplace productivity by addressing mental health challenges through innovative technology solutions. Our mobile and web applications are designed to offer personalized mental health support, making it easier for employees to manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health issues in a confidential and empowering environment.

Job Description:

We are seeking an experienced QA Automation Tester to join our dynamic team. As an Automation Tester, you will play a critical role in ensuring the quality, reliability, and effectiveness of our mobile and web applications. Your expertise in automation scripting and testing methodologies will contribute to providing a seamless, bug-free experience for our users, thereby supporting our mission to enhance workplace mental health and productivity.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and execute automation scripts using JavaScript/TypeScript and tools like Selenium, Appium, or similar.
  • Contribute to the automation framework's architecture and design for mobile and web application testing.
  • Perform thorough manual and automated testing, including functional, regression, and performance testing, to identify bugs and issues.
  • Collaborate with the development team to understand feature requirements and ensure quality standards are met before release
  • Analyze test results, document bugs, and track issues through resolution.
  • Stay updated with new testing tools and test strategies to improve test cycles.

Requirements:

Experience: Minimum 3 years of experience in QA Automation, with a focus on mobile and web applications. Architect/Lead roles may require up to 9 years of experience.

Technical Skills:

  • Proficiency in automation scripting with JavaScript/TypeScript.
  • Solid understanding of programming concepts in Java or Python.
  • Experienced in manual and functional testing with a strong ability to identify system vulnerabilities and inconsistencies.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field..
  • Certifications in software testing (ISTQB, Selenium, Appium, etc.) are a plus.

Soft Skills:

  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Strong communication and collaboration abilities.
  • Passion for improving mental health outcomes through technology.
Benefits:
  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to work in a fast-growing, impactful tech company.
  • Supportive team environment with a focus on personal and professional growth.
  • Access to the latest tools and technologies.
  • Flexible working hours and remote work options.
How to Apply:

Please submit your resume, cover letter, and any relevant work samples or GitHub links showcasing your testing projects to . Include 'QA Automation Tester Application' in the subject line.
